Product Information

AStrutTie is a strut-tie model analysis/design software for concrete members with disturbed stress region(s).
It enables users to design corbel (bracket), abutment/pier footing, bridge pier coping (pier cap), frame corner, anchorage zone, deep beam, etc. The software supports U.S (ACI, AASHTO) and Europe (EuroCode 2) Codes and Specifications.
